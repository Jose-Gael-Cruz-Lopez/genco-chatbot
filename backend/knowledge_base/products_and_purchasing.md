# Products and Purchasing — Home Delivery

## Buying Laundry Detergent Sheets

Generation Conscious sells laundry detergent sheets for home delivery through their online store.

To purchase, visit the product page:
**https://generationconscious.co/product/laundry-detergent-sheets/**

On that page you can:
- Choose your preferred sheet count
- Choose your preferred scent
- Select a one-time purchase or a subscription

Once you have selected your options, add to cart and proceed to checkout.

## Shop Fallback

## Shipping

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout using live USPS rates. The exact shipping amount is shown at checkout — the bot does not quote specific dollar figures.

## Sales Tax

Sales tax applies to orders shipped to New York only. The exact tax amount is shown at checkout.

## Pricing

Exact prices are displayed on the product page and at checkout. Never quote specific dollar figures — direct any pricing questions to the product page or checkout.
