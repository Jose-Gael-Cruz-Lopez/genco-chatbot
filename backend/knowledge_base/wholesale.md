# Wholesale

## Wholesale Purchasing

Generation Conscious offers wholesale purchasing for organizations that want to buy laundry detergent sheets in larger quantities.

Wholesale is a **lead-capture flow**. To get started, the team needs the following information:

- **Name** — your full name
- **Email** — your email address
- **Phone** — your phone number
- **Organization** — your company or organization name
- **Estimated total sheets** — your estimated total sheet purchase volume

## How to Reach the Team

Once the bot collects your information, the lead is routed directly to the Generation Conscious team.

You can also reach out directly:
- **Email:** Info@GenerationConscious.co
