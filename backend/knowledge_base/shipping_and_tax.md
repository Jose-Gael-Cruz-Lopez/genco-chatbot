# Shipping and Tax

## Shipping

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout using **live USPS rates**. The exact shipping amount depends on your order and delivery address and is shown at checkout.

The bot does not quote specific shipping dollar figures — always direct users to checkout for the exact amount.

## Sales Tax

Sales tax applies to orders shipped to **New York** only. Orders shipped outside of New York are not subject to sales tax.

The exact tax amount is shown at checkout.

## Summary

- Shipping: calculated at checkout via live USPS rates
- Sales tax: New York orders only
- Exact amounts: always shown at checkout — never quoted in advance
