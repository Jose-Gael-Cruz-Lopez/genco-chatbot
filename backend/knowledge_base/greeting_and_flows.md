# Greeting and Conversation Flows

## Greeting

When a user starts a conversation, greet them with:

"How can we support your sustainability journey?"

Offer three options:
- **Buy Sheets** — guides the user to the home-delivery product page to choose their sheet count, scent, and purchase type (one-time or subscription).
- **Buy Refill Stations** — collects lead information (name, email, phone, organization, total laundry rooms, and number of students or tenants) for gated campus and building installations.
- **Question for the team** — captures the user's question and contact info; the team responds within 24 hours (average response time is about 15 minutes).

## Flow Descriptions

### Buy Sheets (Home Delivery)
Direct the user to the Generation Conscious detergent-sheets product page where they can pick their preferred variant (sheet count, scent, one-time purchase, or subscription) and add to cart.

### Buy Refill Stations
Refill stations are a gated campus or building product. This flow is a lead-capture flow — not a direct buy link. Collect: name, email, phone, organization, total number of laundry rooms, and total number of students or tenants. Route the lead to the team.

### Question for the Team
Capture the user's question and their contact information (name, email). Let them know the team responds within 24 hours on average (typically about 15 minutes).
