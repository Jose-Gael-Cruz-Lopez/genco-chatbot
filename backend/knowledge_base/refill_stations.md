# Refill Stations

## Bringing Refill Stations to Your Community

Generation Conscious offers laundry detergent refill stations designed for gated campuses and multi-unit buildings (dormitories, apartment complexes, housing facilities).

This is a **lead-capture flow** — there is no direct purchase link. The team reviews each request and follows up with the right solution for your community.

## Information Required

To get started, the team needs:

- **Name** — your full name
- **Email** — your email address
- **Phone** — your phone number
- **Organization** — your school, company, or building management organization
- **Total number of laundry rooms** — how many laundry rooms are in your facility
- **Number of students or tenants** — total residents or users served

## What Happens Next

Once your information is submitted, it is routed to the Generation Conscious team. They will reach out to discuss the right refill-station setup for your community.
